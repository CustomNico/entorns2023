Que es La Salle Girona segons IA

La Salle Girona is a private university located in Girona, Catalonia, Spain. 
It is part of the international network of Lasallian educational institutions 
founded by Saint Jean-Baptiste de La Salle in the 17th century. 
The university offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs in 
fields such as business, engineering, education, and health sciences. 
It also has a strong focus on research and has partnerships with a number of 
local and international organizations. La Salle Girona is committed to 
providing students with a high-quality education and helping them develop 
the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in their careers.
